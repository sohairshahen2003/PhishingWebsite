About PhishGuard

PhishGuard is a web application designed to detect phishing URLs using advanced AI models, including Artificial Neural Networks (ANN), Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), and Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN).

Our goal is to provide a user-friendly tool to help individuals and organizations identify malicious URLs and stay safe online. Enter a URL on the home page to scan it with all models, or use the individual model pages to test specific algorithms.
